How do I get started ?

Geotag-X is still under development, however there are two ways you can volunteer.


Find Photos

Without photos, the applications on this website are not going to give us much information. We need your help to find photos for the different projects that are currently on the website.
To help you do this we are working on an add-on for firefox that will allow you to send photos directly to our server while you are browsing the web

In the meantime...
If you find Flickr albums containing relevant photos send them to us at UNOSAT@UNITAR.ORG and we will load them in for analysis.

Analyse Photos

Help us extract information from the photos being collected by contributing to the different applications on Geotag-X
It's easy...

  • On the homepage choose the project you want to work on.
  • On the project page, select the application you like and get started!
If you are not sure what you are doing Start with the application’s tutorial. And don’t worry if you are not sure about your answers, each photo is presented to at least 30 people and we take the majority answer, so it is ok to make mistakes.

There are lots of resources available on the internet to help you answer the questions. There is no time limit on the applications so you can take time to search for clarification if you are not sure what you are looking at.

What now?
Pick a project on the homepage – or – Find photos


Remember most of these projects are works in progress so you will probably find bugs or things that you don't really like. So we would love to hear what you think , your feedback helps us to improve Geotag-X and the projects. You can post it on GitHub or use the feedback form.
