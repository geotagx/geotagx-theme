GeoTag-X is a research project set up by the United Nations Institute for Training and Research’s Operational Satellite Applications Programme (UNITAR/UNOSAT) as part of the Citizen Cyberlab initiative. It aims to help relief efforts on the ground plan a response by asking volunteers to analyse different forms of media—such as photos or videos—taken in disaster-stricken areas.
