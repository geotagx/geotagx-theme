Contact Us

We welcome collaborators for new GeoTag-X projects such as organisations or researchers addressing a particular crisis. If you have an idea for a project, please send us an email.

Your feedback helps us improve GeoTag-X so we would love to hear what you think about the project. We have set up a feedback page where you can leave your suggestions.

Have you found a bug? GeoTag-X is an open-source project that is constantly being improved. Our code can be found on GitHub; feel free to open an issue.
